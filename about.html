About Me

I’m Viraj Upadhyay, a 17-year-old film enthusiast passionate about cinematography and technology. I’m fascinated by how images evoke emotions and how technology enhances simple shots into cinematic experiences. All the films on this site were shot with a smartphone, showcasing how media software transforms raw footage into polished films.

For me, filmmaking is about capturing mood and storytelling through angles, cuts, and light. I’m excited by how technology can push the boundaries of film production. Quality can be achieved with minimal equipment; it’s the careful selection of shots, shadows, and sounds that creates atmosphere.
